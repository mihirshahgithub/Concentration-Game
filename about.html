Why Monster Match?

Monster Match celebrates Halloween with a walk down terror lane. It rekindles childhood moments of simultaneous terror and joy and sleepless nights with the flashlights under the bedsheets.

Monster Match Rules

Monster Match is simple: pick any two cards and match them with the same horror movie character. If you get it wrong, you will have to start from the beginning. The goal is to match all eighteen cards in the shortest amount of time.
